How much does it cost to build a house in Sri Lanka in 2026?

A standard house in Sri Lanka costs between LKR 7,000 and LKR 15,000 per square foot in 2026. For a 2,000 sqft home, expect to budget between LKR 14 million (standard finish) and LKR 30 million (luxury finish). Budget builds start from LKR 6,000/sqft and premium signature builds exceed LKR 30,000/sqft.

What affects construction cost per square foot in Sri Lanka?

Key cost drivers include: build type (roofed, box-type, villa, mansion), finish level, location (Colombo vs. outstation), design complexity, material prices, and foundation conditions. Box-type / flat-roof houses typically cost 10–15% more per sqft than pitched-roof houses due to waterproofing requirements.
