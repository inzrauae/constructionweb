Who We Are — Gaura Construction

Gaura Construction (Pvt) Ltd is a leading Sri Lanka construction company with over 75 completed projects island-wide. We specialise in residential, commercial and industrial construction, offering Design & Build, renovation, interior design, and MEP services.

This company profile covers our full capabilities — from house construction and luxury villas to commercial complexes and government projects. Browse our construction services or use our free cost calculator to plan your budget.

We serve all 25 districts across Sri Lanka — Colombo, Gampaha, Kandy, Galle, Jaffna and beyond. Every project is backed by qualified engineers, transparent BOQ pricing, and end-to-end project management.
