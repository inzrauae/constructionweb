Systems that support the building

We help align power, water, drainage, and mechanical needs with structure, interiors, and practical maintenance.

What MEP services include

MEP services are the systems that make a building usable and comfortable. Mechanical, electrical, and plumbing work must be coordinated with structure, ceilings, partitions, finishes, and equipment so the final building performs well and remains maintainable. Poor coordination in this stage often creates major rework later.

Electrical Services

Power distribution, lighting coordination, switch layouts, conduit planning, and safe electrical routing.

Plumbing Services

Water supply, drainage planning, sanitary coordination, and service lines that support long-term reliability.

Mechanical Services

Mechanical integration for ventilation and building service requirements depending on project type and scope.

Why MEP coordination matters

  • Prevents clashes between structure, ceilings, walls, and service lines.
  • Improves usability, safety, and maintenance access after handover.
  • Supports better cost control by avoiding late system changes.
  • Helps renovation and fitout projects move with less disruption.

Where MEP services are used

  • New house construction and residential developments.
  • Office and commercial interior fitouts.
  • Renovation and modernization projects.
  • Retail spaces, mixed-use buildings, and selected specialty scopes.

Frequently asked questions

  • What does MEP stand for? It stands for mechanical, electrical, and plumbing services within a building.
  • Can you provide MEP services for residential projects? Yes. Gaura Construction supports MEP works for homes and residential construction in Sri Lanka.
  • Do MEP services need to be planned early? Yes. Early coordination helps avoid clashes, rework, and hidden cost issues later in the project.
  • Can MEP be combined with interiors and renovation work? Yes. In many projects, it should be coordinated with those scopes from the start.
