Common construction vacancies we may hire for

Openings depend on active projects, but these are the most common construction job vacancy categories candidates search for in Sri Lanka.

Technical roles

  • Site Engineer
  • Technical Officer

  • Project coordination assistant

Site supervision roles

Trade and labour roles

What makes a strong applicant

Searchers often want a clear answer to this question, so here is the short version.

  • Relevant construction experience or a willingness to learn quickly.
  • Attendance, punctuality, and safe work habits.
  • Ability to follow drawings, instructions, and site supervisor guidance.
  • Good communication and dependable work quality.

Frequently asked questions

Are there always open vacancies?

Not always. Openings depend on project schedules, but this page is the best place to start when searching for construction job vacancy opportunities with Gaura Construction.

Do I need construction experience?

Experience helps, but dependable entry-level applicants can also be considered for suitable site support and labour roles.

Can I apply from any district?

Yes. Candidates from across Sri Lanka can apply, especially if they are willing to work on project locations where support is needed.

What should I include in my application?

Include your name, phone number, district, role applied for, years of experience, and any relevant site references or certificates.
